# Market Cycles, Wyckoff Method & Altcoin Rotation

## WYCKOFF MARKET CYCLE

The market moves in four repeating phases. Identifying the current phase determines strategy.

---

### Phase 1: ACCUMULATION

**What is it?**
Institutional investors quietly buying large positions without driving price up. They absorb sell pressure from retail panic sellers (usually after a major downtrend).

**How to identify:**
- Price has been in a prolonged downtrend
- Price reaches a "Selling Climax" (SC) — massive volume, panic selling, price crashes fast
- Followed by an "Automatic Rally" (AR) — price bounces sharply off the lows
- Then "Secondary Test" (ST) — price comes back down to test the SC low with LESS volume
- Then a long period of sideways ranging between SC low and AR high
- Range forms with multiple tests of support (Spring) and resistance (Upthrust)

**Key signs:**
- Volume dries up on downward moves (sellers exhausted)
- Volume picks up on upward moves (demand absorbing supply)
- Fakeout below the range low (Spring) = strong buy signal
- Price closes back above the range = Accumulation complete

**Strategy:** Accumulate long positions during the Spring. Ride the markup phase.

**Crypto Example:** BTC bottoming in 2018–2019 at $3,200 area. Then 2020 accumulation before the run to $69K.

---

### Phase 2: MARKUP (Bull Market)

**What is it?**
Institutions' positions are now in profit. Price trends upward as word spreads, retail joins in.

**How to identify:**
- Clear uptrend: Higher Highs, Higher Lows
- Pullbacks are shallow and bought quickly
- Volume increases on up-moves, decreases on pullbacks
- News turns positive, media coverage increases
- New all-time highs being made

**Stages within Markup:**
1. **Early Markup**: Quiet uptrend, most retail still skeptical
2. **Mid Markup**: Acceleration, breakout to new highs, FOMO begins
3. **Late Markup**: Parabolic move, mainstream media, extreme greed

**Strategy:**
- Enter on pullbacks to key support in early/mid markup
- Use trailing stops in late markup — reduce position size
- NEVER chase late-stage parabolic moves

**Crypto Cycle Note:** BTC leads the markup. Alt Season follows 2–6 months after BTC makes new ATH.

---

### Phase 3: DISTRIBUTION

**What is it?**
Institutions quietly SELLING their accumulated positions to retail investors who are now euphoric. Price appears strong but is secretly being sold.

**How to identify:**
- Price reaches major highs, volume is very high but price is NOT going up significantly
- "Buying Climax" (BC) — price spikes up on high volume but quickly reverses
- Multiple tests of the high with declining volume = distribution
- "Upthrust After Distribution" (UTAD) — fakeout above range then collapses
- Narrowing range at the top (choppiness)
- News is extremely positive but price stops responding

**Key signs:**
- Divergence on RSI/MACD while price at highs
- Funding rates extremely positive (everyone long)
- Fear & Greed in Extreme Greed zone (>85)
- Retail is "all in", social media full of price predictions

**Strategy:** Reduce long positions. Look for short entry after UTAD. Stop adding new longs.

**Red flags in crypto:** Influencers shilling everything, "this time different" narratives, altcoins 10x–100x in weeks.

---

### Phase 4: MARKDOWN (Bear Market)

**What is it?**
Price trends downward. Institutions are out (or short). Retail holders suffer.

**How to identify:**
- Clear downtrend: Lower Highs, Lower Lows
- Rallies are sharp but quickly sold
- Volume picks up on down-moves
- News turns negative, media declares crypto/asset "dead"
- Leverage liquidations cascade (especially in crypto)

**Stages:**
1. **Early Markdown**: Denial phase, people "buy the dip"
2. **Mid Markdown**: Reality sets in, forced selling begins
3. **Late Markdown**: Capitulation, panic selling, extreme fear

**Strategy:**
- Short rallies to resistance in early/mid markdown
- Look for accumulation signs in late markdown to position for next cycle
- Never average down into a falling knife

## BITCOIN-SPECIFIC CYCLE

### Halving Cycle Framework
Bitcoin halving occurs approximately every 4 years (210,000 blocks). After each halving, supply of new BTC is cut in half — historically triggers bull runs.

| Phase | Timing (relative to halving) | Price Behavior |
|-------|------------------------------|----------------|
| Pre-Halving Accumulation | 12–18 months before halving | Slow accumulation, choppy |
| Pre-Halving Rally | 3–6 months before halving | +50–100% run, then pullback |
| Post-Halving Consolidation | 0–3 months after halving | Choppy, tests patience |
| Post-Halving Bull Run | 3–18 months after halving | Main bull market (+300–1000%) |
| Distribution Top | ~12–18 months after halving | Euphoria, all-time highs |
| Bear Market | 12–24 months after distribution | -70 to -85% drawdown typical |

### BTC Cycle Indicators
- **Pi Cycle Top Indicator**: When 111-day MA crosses 350-day MA × 2 from below = market top signal (historically precise)
- **200-week MA**: Has never been broken in a bear market close — acts as ultimate support
- **MVRV Z-Score**: >7 = overvalued, sell zone | <0 = undervalued, buy zone
- **Puell Multiple**: >4 = miner sell pressure, near top | <0.5 = miner capitulation, near bottom

## ALTCOIN ROTATION GUIDE

### The Rotation Order (how money typically flows)
1. **BTC rallies first** — BTC dominance rises
2. **ETH outperforms** — ETH/BTC ratio rises
3. **Large-cap alts follow** (SOL, BNB, XRP, ADA) — after BTC/ETH establish direction
4. **Mid-cap alts pump** — 2–4x moves on confirmed altcoin season
5. **Small-cap/narrative tokens** — biggest gains but most dangerous (DePIN, AI, GameFi, memes)
6. **Memecoins get insane** — absolute top signal, extreme greed everywhere

### Altcoin Season Index
- 75–100: Altcoin Season (75%+ of top 50 alts outperforming BTC last 90 days)
- 25–75: Mixed market, select alts performing
- 0–25: Bitcoin Season (BTC dominates)

### BTC Dominance (BTC.D) as Trading Signal
| BTC.D Reading | Implication | Strategy |
|---------------|-------------|----------|
| Rising (50% → 55%+) | Money flowing TO Bitcoin | Buy BTC, reduce alts |
| Falling (55% → 45%) | Alt season starting | Buy strong alts vs BTC |
| Near 60%+ | BTC very dominant | Almost no alt season yet |
| Near 40% | Peak alt season territory | Near-term rotation back to BTC |

### How to Find Sector Leaders
1. Find the narrative gaining attention (AI coins, RWA, DePIN, etc.)
2. Find the STRONGEST performer in that sector (highest relative strength)
3. Buy the sector leader, not the laggard (laggards catch up last, fall first)
4. Exit when narrative loses momentum and money rotates to next sector

### ETH/BTC Ratio
- ETH/BTC rising = healthy alt season incoming
- ETH/BTC falling = Bitcoin dominance, stay in BTC/stable
- Key level: If ETH/BTC breaks above major resistance = confirmed alt season signal

## MARKET SENTIMENT TOOLS

### Fear & Greed Index (Crypto)
- **0–10: Extreme Fear** → Historically one of the best buying opportunities
- **11–25: Fear** → Good accumulation zone, be selective
- **26–46: Neutral** → Wait for direction
- **47–74: Greed** → Caution, reduce new entries
- **75–100: Extreme Greed** → High probability of correction, reduce exposure, take profits

**Contrarian Rule:** Buy when others are fearful. Sell when others are greedy. (Buffett principle applied to crypto.)

### Crypto Market Health Checklist
Before any large position — score each:
- [ ] BTC above 200-week MA → +1
- [ ] BTC.D trending below 55% → +1 (for alts) or above 55% trending up → +1 (for BTC)
- [ ] ETH/BTC ratio rising → +1
- [ ] Fear & Greed below 50 → +1
- [ ] Funding rates neutral/negative → +1
- [ ] Exchange outflows increasing → +1
- [ ] Total market cap above key MAs → +1

**Score 5–7**: Strong conditions for long entries
**Score 3–4**: Neutral, selective entries only
**Score 0–2**: High risk environment, reduce exposure or stay flat
