# Candlestick Patterns Encyclopedia

## REVERSAL PATTERNS — BULLISH

### Single Candle
- **Hammer**: Small body, long lower wick (2x body). At support = strong reversal.
- **Inverted Hammer**: Small body, long upper wick. At support = potential reversal.
- **Dragonfly Doji**: Open=Close=High, long lower wick. Very bullish at support.
- **Bullish Marubozu**: Large green candle, no wicks. Strong buying pressure.
- **Spinning Top (at support)**: Small body, equal wicks. Indecision → watch next candle.

### Two Candle
- **Bullish Engulfing**: Large green candle completely engulfs prior red candle. Strong.
- **Bullish Harami**: Small green candle inside large red candle. Momentum slowing.
- **Tweezer Bottom**: Two candles with same low. Double rejection of level.
- **Piercing Line**: Green candle closes above midpoint of prior red candle.

### Three Candle
- **Morning Star**: Red → Small candle (doji/star) → Large Green. Very reliable.
- **Morning Doji Star**: Same but middle = doji. Strongest version.
- **Three White Soldiers**: Three consecutive green candles, each closing higher. Powerful trend start.
- **Three Inside Up**: Bearish harami → confirming green candle.

## REVERSAL PATTERNS — BEARISH

### Single Candle
- **Shooting Star**: Small body, long upper wick (2x body). At resistance = reversal.
- **Hanging Man**: Same as hammer BUT at resistance = bearish.
- **Gravestone Doji**: Open=Close=Low, long upper wick. Very bearish at resistance.
- **Bearish Marubozu**: Large red candle, no wicks. Strong selling pressure.

### Two Candle
- **Bearish Engulfing**: Large red candle completely engulfs prior green candle. Strong.
- **Bearish Harami**: Small red candle inside large green candle. Momentum slowing.
- **Tweezer Top**: Two candles with same high. Double rejection.
- **Dark Cloud Cover**: Red candle closes below midpoint of prior green candle.

### Three Candle
- **Evening Star**: Green → Small candle → Large Red. Very reliable top signal.
- **Three Black Crows**: Three consecutive red candles, each closing lower. Powerful.
- **Three Inside Down**: Bullish harami → confirming red candle.

## CONTINUATION PATTERNS

### Bullish Continuation
- **Rising Three Methods**: Long green, three small reds (within range), large green. Trend continues.
- **Upside Tasuki Gap**: Gap up, correction candle doesn't fill gap. Keep going up.
- **Mat Hold**: Similar to rising three, gap maintained.

### Bearish Continuation  
- **Falling Three Methods**: Long red, three small greens (within range), large red.
- **Downside Tasuki Gap**: Gap down, correction doesn't fill gap. Continue down.

## INDECISION PATTERNS (Context Dependent)
- **Doji**: Open ≈ Close. At extremes = reversal. In trend = pause then continue.
- **Long-Legged Doji**: Extreme indecision. Major battle between bulls/bears.
- **Four-Price Doji**: All prices equal. Very rare, thin/illiquid market.

## RELIABILITY RANKINGS (at key levels)
1. ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ Morning/Evening Star, Three White Soldiers/Black Crows
2. ⭐⭐⭐⭐ Bullish/Bearish Engulfing, Hammer/Shooting Star (at levels)
3. ⭐⭐⭐ Harami, Tweezer Top/Bottom
4. ⭐⭐ Single Doji, Spinning Top
5. ⭐ Isolated patterns without confirmation

## IMPORTANT RULES FOR PATTERN TRADING
1. **Context matters**: Pattern at key S/R = valid. Random pattern in middle of range = ignore.
2. **Confirmation**: Always wait for close of the candle before entering.
3. **Volume confirmation**: High volume on reversal candle = more reliable.
4. **HTF alignment**: Pattern in direction of higher TF trend = higher probability.
5. **Wick rejection**: Long wicks at key levels = strong rejection, often better signal than the pattern body.
