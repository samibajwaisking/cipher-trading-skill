# Crypto On-Chain & Sentiment Metrics

## Exchange Flow Analysis
- **Exchange Inflows ↑**: Coins moving TO exchanges → Selling pressure incoming → Bearish
- **Exchange Outflows ↑**: Coins leaving exchanges → HODLing / self-custody → Bullish
- **Net Flow**: Outflows > Inflows = accumulation phase

## Funding Rates (Futures)
- **Positive Funding**: Longs paying shorts → Overleveraged longs → Risk of short squeeze reversal
- **Negative Funding**: Shorts paying longs → Overleveraged shorts → Risk of short squeeze (price pump)
- **Extreme levels**: ±0.1% per 8h = crowded trade, fade opportunity
- **Near zero**: Balanced market, safer to trade with trend

## Open Interest (OI)
- **OI Rising + Price Rising**: New longs entering → Bullish confirmation
- **OI Rising + Price Falling**: New shorts entering → Bearish confirmation  
- **OI Falling + Price Rising**: Short squeeze / deleveraging → Less sustainable
- **OI Falling + Price Falling**: Long liquidations / deleveraging

## Liquidation Heatmaps
- Clusters of liquidations above price = Target for price to sweep up (hunts stops)
- Clusters below price = Target for price to sweep down (liquidity grab)
- Large liquidation cluster = Magnet for price

## Fear & Greed Index
- 0–25: Extreme Fear → Historically good buying zone
- 25–45: Fear → Accumulation zone
- 45–55: Neutral
- 55–75: Greed → Caution, reduce new longs
- 75–100: Extreme Greed → High risk of correction, consider taking profit

## BTC Dominance (BTC.D)
- **BTC.D Rising**: Money flowing to BTC from alts → Altcoins underperform
- **BTC.D Falling**: Alt season potential → Alts outperform BTC
- Key levels: Watch 50%, 55%, 60% as major inflection points

## Whale Activity
- Whale accumulation (large wallets growing): Bullish signal
- Whale distribution (large wallets shrinking): Bearish signal
- Large OTC blocks: Detected via exchange reserve sudden drops

## SOPR (Spent Output Profit Ratio)
- >1: Holders selling at profit → Potential resistance
- <1: Holders selling at loss → Potential support / capitulation

## NUPL (Net Unrealized Profit/Loss)
- Euphoria/Greed zone: >0.75 — Near cycle top territory
- Optimism zone: 0.5–0.75 — Bull market healthy
- Belief/Denial: 0.25–0.5
- Capitulation: <0 — Near cycle bottom territory

## Key On-Chain Signals for Entry/Exit
**Buy signals:**
- Exchange outflows increasing
- Funding negative (shorts overcrowded)
- Fear & Greed in Fear/Extreme Fear
- Whale accumulation detected

**Sell/Caution signals:**
- Exchange inflows increasing sharply
- Funding highly positive (longs overcrowded)
- Fear & Greed in Extreme Greed
- Large profit-taking by long-term holders

## Derivatives Market Deep Dive

### Liquidation Cascades
- Large liquidation cluster at a level = price magnet
- When price approaches a liquidation cluster, momentum often accelerates
- After a large liquidation event (cascade): often a sharp reversal — the fuel is gone
- Tools to check: Coinglass liquidation heatmap

### Long/Short Ratio
- >60% longs: Crowded long trade, short squeeze risk lower but long squeeze (dump) risk higher
- >60% shorts: Crowded short trade, short squeeze potential (sharp pump to liquidate shorts)
- Near 50/50: Balanced market, move determined by external catalyst

### Perpetual Futures Basis
- Spot price vs futures price gap
- Futures trading at significant premium to spot = overheated, correction likely
- Futures at discount to spot (backwardation) = bearish sentiment, potential bottom forming

## Stablecoin Flow Analysis

### Stablecoin Market Cap
- Rising stablecoin market cap = dry powder sitting on sidelines = potential buying power
- Falling stablecoin market cap = capital deployed into crypto = market may be overextended

### Tether (USDT) Printing
- Large USDT mint → historically preceded price rises within days/weeks
- Interpretation: Tether printed to buy = institutional buying incoming

### Stablecoin Ratio (Crypto market cap / Stablecoin market cap)
- High ratio: Most capital already deployed, less room for further upside
- Low ratio: Most capital in stables, significant buying potential

## NVT Ratio (Network Value to Transactions)
- Like P/E ratio for Bitcoin
- NVT = Market Cap / Daily Transaction Volume
- High NVT (>150): Overvalued relative to usage → bearish signal
- Low NVT (<40): Undervalued relative to usage → bullish signal
