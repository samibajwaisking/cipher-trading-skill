# Economic Calendar & News Trading Guide

## High-Impact Events (Avoid trading 30 min before/after)

### USD (Most Market-Moving)
- **NFP** (Non-Farm Payrolls): First Friday of month, 8:30 AM EST
  - >200K = Bullish USD | <150K = Bearish USD
- **CPI** (Consumer Price Index): Monthly, 8:30 AM EST
  - Higher than expected = Bullish USD (hawkish Fed pressure)
- **FOMC Meeting**: 8 per year, 2:00 PM EST
  - Rate hike = Bullish USD | Rate cut = Bearish USD
  - Fed Chair press conference = Extreme volatility
- **GDP**: Quarterly, 8:30 AM EST
- **PPI, PCE, Retail Sales, ISM**: Secondary but still market-moving

### EUR Movers
- **ECB Rate Decision**: 8 per year — Major EUR mover
- **German CPI, GDP, PMI**: Strong Germany = Strong EUR
- **EU CPI Flash Estimate**: Monthly

### GBP Movers
- **BOE Rate Decision**: 8 per year
- **UK CPI, GDP, PMI**
- **GBP/JPY Warning**: Most volatile major pair — nicknamed "Widow Maker"

### JPY Movers
- **BOJ Rate Decision**: Ultra-dovish historically — any change = massive JPY move
- **Japanese CPI, GDP**
- **Yen Intervention Risk**: BOJ/MOF can intervene directly

## News Trading Rules
1. **Never trade 30 min before** high-impact news
2. **Wait for initial spike** to fade before entering (first candle after news = emotional)
3. **Buy the rumor, sell the news**: Often price reverses AFTER news confirms expectations
4. **Surprise factor matters**: Actual vs Forecast is what moves markets, not just the number
5. **Straddle strategy**: Only for experienced traders — place buy+sell orders 10 pips above/below current price before news

## DXY (Dollar Index) Correlation
- DXY ↑ = EUR/USD ↓, GBP/USD ↓, Gold ↓, BTC sometimes ↓
- DXY ↓ = EUR/USD ↑, GBP/USD ↑, Gold ↑, Risk assets ↑
- **Most important**: When DXY breaks major level, trade in direction against USD pairs

## COT Report (Commitments of Traders)
- Released every Friday for prior Tuesday data
- **Non-commercials (Speculators)**: Their positioning predicts trend
- Extreme long positioning = Contrarian sell signal (who's left to buy?)
- Extreme short positioning = Contrarian buy signal
- Flip in positioning = Major trend change incoming
